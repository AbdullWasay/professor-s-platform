# The Professor's Website Documentation

This document explains the complete website in simple, non-technical language so it can be shared directly with the client.

## 1) Overall Website Purpose

The website presents **The Professor's** as a premium trading community where members can:
- learn through lectures,
- follow trade ideas,
- chat with the teacher and members,
- and join with a two-month free trial.

The full experience has two parts:
- **Public website** (marketing + information),
- **Member area** (private dashboard after sign-in).

## 2) Public Website (What Visitors See First)

### Homepage

The homepage is structured into clear sections:

- **Hero Section**
  - Main brand message: "Trade smarter, together."
  - Introduces the value proposition and trial offer.
  - Primary call to action: start free trial.

- **Features Section**
  - Explains what is included:
    - live lectures,
    - trade ideas,
    - private chat,
    - community lounge,
    - searchable history,
    - curated member experience.

- **How It Works Section**
  - Simple 3-step explanation:
    1. Create account
    2. Enter rooms
    3. Learn/trade/repeat

- **Pricing Section**
  - One clear offer:
    - 2-month free trial
    - then $30/month
  - Includes what is covered in membership.

- **Contact Section**
  - Built directly into the homepage.
  - Shows support details (email, phone, hours).
  - Includes a contact form with:
    - name,
    - email,
    - phone number,
    - message,
    - prominent "Send message" button.

- **Footer**
  - Brand summary,
  - quick navigation links,
  - legal links (Terms, Privacy),
  - newsletter-style notify input.

### Top Navigation

The top menu allows quick jumps to:
- Features,
- How it works,
- Pricing,
- Contact.

It also includes:
- **Sign in** button,
- **Start free** button.

## 3) Supporting Public Pages

These pages provide trust and compliance information:

- **About**
  - Introduces the community mission and founder story.

- **Terms & Conditions**
  - Explains usage expectations and subscription terms.

- **Privacy Policy**
  - Explains what user data is collected and how it is handled.

## 4) Account Entry Pages

- **Sign Up Page**
  - New users create an account and start the free trial.

- **Sign In Page**
  - Existing users return to their member dashboard.

These pages are focused and streamlined for quick entry.

## 5) Member Dashboard (Private Area)

After sign-in, users access a multi-section dashboard designed like a private community platform.

- **Main Forum**
  - Core content feed with lectures, trade ideas, and discussions.
  - Search and reply functionality.

- **Community Lounge**
  - Casual member chat room for open conversation.

- **Messages**
  - Private direct messaging between members and teacher.

- **Subscription**
  - Shows current plan/trial status.
  - Allows upgrade to paid membership.

- **About (Member Version)**
  - In-dashboard brand story and community values.

- **Contact (Member Version)**
  - In-dashboard contact page with support form/details.

## 6) Membership and Access Logic (Client-Friendly View)

- Visitors can browse public marketing content freely.
- Private dashboard content is intended for signed-in users.
- The trial period is highlighted throughout the journey.
- After trial end, the experience encourages upgrade to remain active.

## 7) Brand Experience Summary

The website is designed to feel:
- premium,
- focused,
- community-first,
- and conversion-oriented.

It balances:
- strong landing-page storytelling,
- clear pricing and trust pages,
- and a full private member environment.

- and all major dashboard areas.
